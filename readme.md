Airline flight analysis using hive and pig

There are 29 columns in this dataset. Some of them are:

Year: 1987 – 2008
Month: 1 – 12
FlightNum: Flight number
Canceled: Was the flight canceled?
CancelleationCode: The reason for cancellation.


ANALYSIS:
1.Which month have seen the most number of cancellation due to bad weather?

2.Top 10 route(origin and dest) that has seen maximum diversions?
3.Top 5 visited destination
